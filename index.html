Blue Poison Dart Frog

  • Scientific Name: D. tinctorius “azureus”
  • Average Length: 3-4.5cm
  • Average Lifespan: 5-7 years
  • Habitat: tropical forests

The blue poison dart frog is found in the forests surrounded by the Sipaliwini savanna, which is located in southern Suriname and adjacent far northern Brazil.
